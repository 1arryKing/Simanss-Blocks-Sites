PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2026

WHO WE ARE

Simanss Blocks is a concrete block manufacturer based in Kumasi, Ghana. We operate the website at this domain and can be reached at simanssblocks@gmail.com.

WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us through our forms:

  • Quote requests: first name, last name, email address, phone number, block type, and project details.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: email address only.

We do not collect any information automatically beyond what your browser sends as part of a normal web request (such as your IP address and browser type), and we do not use tracking pixels or fingerprinting.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To respond to your quote requests and provide pricing or product information.
  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to, including product updates and industry insights.
  • To contact you regarding your order or enquiry.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our forms are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to us by email. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to how they handle that data in transit.

DATA RETENTION

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfil your request or maintain our business relationship. If you would like us to delete your data, contact us at simanssblocks@gmail.com and we will action your request within 14 days.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by contacting us.

CONTACT

For any privacy-related questions, email us at simanssblocks@gmail.com or call +233 05 505 48806.
