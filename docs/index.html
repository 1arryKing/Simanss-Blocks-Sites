QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our blocks and services

We manufacture four block types: 5-inch solid, 5-inch hollow, 6-inch solid and 6-inch hollow — all at GH¢8 per block. Solid blocks suit foundations and load-bearing walls; hollow blocks suit partitions and lighter structures. We'll advise the right mix for your design free of charge.
We deliver to Effiduase, Juaben, Ejisu, Konongo and Kumasi. Blocks in stock are usually delivered within 1–3 days; large orders are produced and cured to schedule, so contact us early to plan deliveries around your work programme. Delivery charges depend on distance and quantity, and you can also collect from our yard in Effiduase.
We produce the four standard types used across Ghana's construction industry (5" and 6", solid and hollow). For unusual requirements, talk to us — if we can't produce it, we'll tell you honestly and point you to someone who can.
Every batch is mixed to a strict cement-to-aggregate ratio, machine-vibrated during moulding, water-cured for a minimum of 21 days and inspected before sale — and our written supply contract includes a 48-hour replacement guarantee for any defective block. Visit the yard any time and test a block yourself.
Yes — volume discounts are available on orders above 2,000 blocks. Contact us with your quantity and site location for a written quotation.
Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash or AT Money — all on +233 55 054 8806), bank transfer, or cash with a printed receipt. Orders start production on a 50% deposit, with the balance due before or on delivery. Every payment gets a written receipt.
